Data Scientist – Machine Learning

About the Role:
The Data Scientist will demonstrate strong knowledge of machine learning algorithms
and lead projects to build AI-assisted marketing analytics solutions.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement AI/ML solutions for marketing analytics
- Work with cross-functional teams to deliver data-driven insights
- Build and deploy ML models for real-world business problems
- Engage with stakeholders and customers to understand requirements

Required Skills:
- Machine Learning algorithms: Linear Regression, Logistic Regression, Clustering, Decision Trees, Random Forest, GBM, DNN, Naive Bayes, SVM
- Python, SQL, PySpark
- Pandas, NumPy, SciPy, Scikit-Learn
- Statistical analysis and data modeling
- Data visualization and storytelling
- Strong analytical and critical thinking skills
- Calculus, Linear Algebra, Statistics, Probability

Education:
- Master’s degree in Data Science, Statistics, Math, Computer Science, or related quantitative field
